About App

Suraksha QR

Suraksha QR is a smart emergency safety system designed to help people during accidents or emergencies. By scanning a QR code placed on a vehicle, anyone can quickly access important information about the vehicle owner.

How It Works

📱 User creates a profile in the Suraksha QR app.
🧾 Important details and emergency contacts are stored securely.
📷 A QR code is generated for the vehicle.
🚨 In case of an accident, anyone can scan the QR code.
📞 Emergency contacts and medical details become visible instantly.

Key Features

👤 Vehicle Owner Information
📞 Emergency Family Contact
🩸 Blood Group & Medical Notes
📄 Important Documents Access
📍 Location Based Emergency Support

Purpose

The goal of Suraksha QR is to save time during emergencies and help first responders quickly contact family members and access important information.
